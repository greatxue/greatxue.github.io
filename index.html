Welcome to “Fantasy of Poseidon”, my personal blog!

I’m Kevin Shuey, a passionate student of quantitative finance with a wide range of interests and a love for exploration. In this blog, I will share my knowledge and insights on various topics, including love, life, literature, computer science, and quantitative finance.

I believe that every aspect of life is worth contemplating, exploring, and sharing. Whether it’s the sweetness and challenges of romantic relationships, the intricacies of everyday life, or the wonders of literature and computer science, I aim to establish a deep connection and meaningful exchange with my readers through my writing.

Literature is another world that resides within me. I avidly read across different genres and draw inspiration from these literary adventures. Through my blog, I will share my experiences, recommend captivating works, and eagerly await reading suggestions and shared booklists from my readers.

Computers and technology have an irresistible allure for me. I am captivated by the ever-evolving realm of computer science. I will delve into various aspects of this field, share insights, discuss new trends, and explore the intersection of technology and quantitative finance.

Join me on this journey of discovery and enlightenment as we explore the realms of love, life, literature, computer science, and quantitative finance together. Together, we will unravel the mysteries and embrace the wonders that await us in the “Fantasy of Poseidon.”
